Per me perdor thymeleaf duhet me perdor thymeleafin si dependency ne pom xml

Masi qe te boj import dependencyn e krijoj ni html file edhe te pjesa nalt
<html lang="en" xmlns:th="http://www.thymeleaf.org">
DOMETHAN QIT PJESEN (xmlns:th="http://www.thymeleaf.org")

Nese du me paraqit tash ni model qe e kom marr prej metodes ne model e perdori tagun

<h2 th:text="${emri i modeli qe e perdorum}"> </h2>

Kur te perdorim thymeleafin pjesa mrena h2 nuk funksionon

Nese dojna me bo concat

<h2 th:text="${'Welcome' + student.get}"> </h2>
